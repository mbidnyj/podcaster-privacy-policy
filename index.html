Privacy Policy for Podcaster

Last updated: 21.02.2024

No Personal Data Collection

The Podcaster app is dedicated to protecting your privacy. We do not collect or store any personal data from our users. Our service is designed to provide AI-generated podcasts through the use of OpenAI's ChatGPT API, which is responsible for generating the podcast content based on user-provided topics.

Collection of Topics

We collect the topics that users input to create podcasts. This information is used exclusively for the purpose of fine-tuning and adjusting the quality of the podcasts generated. These topics are not associated with any personal data and are not used for any other purpose beyond improving the content generation for our users.

Use of Third-Party Services

Our app utilizes OpenAI's ChatGPT API as a third-party service to generate podcast content. However, OpenAI does not use the data submitted to the API for any other purpose than to provide the service. We ensure that no personal data is sent to OpenAI or any other third parties through the use of our app.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, please do not hesitate to contact us at mbidnyj@gmail.com.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Therefore, we recommend reviewing this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
